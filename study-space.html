About Study Space

Your all-in-one productivity sanctuary designed to help students focus, manage tasks, and reach academic excellence.

What is Study Space?

Study Space is a beautifully crafted student productivity dashboard that brings together everything you need to excel academically. From task management and pomodoro timing to relaxing study music and progress tracking — all in one seamless interface. Built for focused learners who want to make the most of every study session.

Task Management

Create, complete, and organize your study tasks with priority tagging and local storage persistence.

Pomodoro Timer

Built-in Pomodoro technique with 25-min focus, 5-min short breaks, and 15-min long breaks.

Study Music

Curated ambient sounds — lofi beats, rain focus, and calm piano — to keep you in the zone.

Progress Tracking

Log and visualize your weekly study hours with dynamic Chart.js bar charts.

Dark Mode

Switch between light and dark themes to protect your eyes during late-night study sessions.

Local Privacy

All your data stays on your device using localStorage — no accounts, no servers, no data leaks.
